About

Behind the lens

I'm Parth, a nature photographer based in Ahmedabad, India. My work centers on landscapes, clouds, and the quiet drama of natural light — highland meadows at golden hour, shifting skies, and the kind of stillness you only find outdoors.

When I'm not shooting, I'm exploring how AI-assisted tools can extend a photographer's creative workflow, from editing to ideation.
